ABOUT OUR MISSION

Our primary mission is save trees and help poor people. We all can help each other by sharing our books, novels and other stuffs. You also can take books, novels and stuffs from others.

We have created this system in which you all can register and add you books , novels and other stuffs. You can also create your library and make visible to others. Other can search books and send a request to share that book with them. You also can reject or accept her/his request. If you accept this request and share that particular book then our mission will be accomplished. You can also share other previous year school/college books to share with needy people.

ABOUT "MARUTSAKHA"

Marutsakha is a nonprofit organization. Which helps struggles to build their career. We build products for our country and world. We also create different framworks to make others work easy. If you want to help us please donate some money. Click on Contact link to connect with us.
